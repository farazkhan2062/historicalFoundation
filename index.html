Pre‑Pakistan Education (712–1947)

Maktabs and Madrasahs taught Qur’an, Arabic, Persian, mathematics and science. Mughal era saw educational growth; British period introduced modern schooling.

Sir Syed Ahmed Khan

Sir Syed promoted modern scientific education and founded MAO College (Aligarh), laying the foundation for Muslim educational revival.

Education After Independence

Pakistan focused on literacy, commissions, HEC establishment, and Article 25‑A ensuring free education.

Bhutto Era Education (1972–1977)

Education was nationalized and declared free. Enrollment rose but quality and management challenges emerged.
